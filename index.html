About Us

Our Mission

Voice of Students India was founded in 2020 with the vision to create a platform where students from all across India can voice their concerns, share ideas, and work together to improve the education system.

We believe that students are not just passive recipients of education but active stakeholders who should have a say in policies that affect their learning journey.

Our organization works at the grassroots level to identify issues faced by students, conduct research, and advocate for policy changes at both institutional and governmental levels.
